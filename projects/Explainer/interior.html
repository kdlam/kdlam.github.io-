PRANAMASANA

PRAYER POSE

Instructions

Stand at the edge of your yoga mat with feet together. Expand your chest and relax your shoulders. Inhale and lift both arms up from the sides. Exhale and bring your palms together in front of the chest in prayer position.

Benefits:
Establishes a state of humility, gratitude, concentration and calmness. Helps maintain the balance of the body. Relaxes the nervous system.

HASTA UTTANSA

RAISED ARM POSE

Instructions

Inhale and lift the arms up and back, keeping the biceps close to the ears. Feel the full stretch from the heels to the tips of the fingers.

Benefits:
Stretches abdominal and intestinal muscles, arms and spinal cord.Expands the chest resulting in the full intake of oxygen.

HASTA PADASANA

HAND TO FOOT POSE

Instructions

While breathing out, stretch forward and bend downwards, isolating the waist. Exhale and bring the hands down to the floor next to the feet.

Benefits:
Tones the abdomen and leg muscles, stretches spine and back muscles, and opens the hips and shoulders.

ASHWA SANCHALANASANA

EQUESTRIAN POSE

Instructions

Breathing in, push your right leg back and bring the right knee to the floor. Look up and place the left foot is in between the palms.

Benefits
Tones abdomen, muscles of thighs and legs. Relieves spine and neck tention. Helps indigestion, constipation, and sciatica.

DANDASANA PHALAKASANA

PLANK POSE

Instructions

As you breathe in, take the left leg back and bring the whole body in a straight line. Keep your arms perpendicular to the floor.

Benefits
Strengthens arm, back and abdominal muscles. Improves spinal column and cord issues. Calms the mind.

BHUJANGASANA

COBRA POSE

Instructions

Slide forward and raise the chest up into the Cobra posture. Keep the shoulders away from the ears. Look up. While inhaling, gently push the chest forward. Exhale, and gently push the navel down.

Benefits
Sends the adrenal glands and kidneys richer supply of blood. Tones ovaries, uterus and liver. Strengthens entire upper body, and relieves menstrual irregularities and constipation.
