My Music Preference Throughout the Day

Above is data taken from a week of listening to music. It is my attempt at understanding whether there was a relationship between the time of day and what genre of music I listen to. My hypothesis coming into this was that the time of day typically indicates what kind of activity I would be doing, and perhaps, also the mood I was feeling. For instance, mornings are when I typically go to the gym. Does it make sense that I listen to more upbeat music like pop and rap during the morning with this in mind?
